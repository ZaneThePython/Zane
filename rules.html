Community Rules

Please follow these guidelines to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience on Zane.org.

  • 1. Be respectful: Treat others with kindness. Hate speech, bullying, or harassment will not be tolerated.
  • 2. No spamming: Do not flood posts with excessive messages or advertisements.
  • 3. Keep it appropriate: No NSFW content, extreme violence, or illegal activity.
  • 4. No impersonation: Do not pretend to be someone else, including moderators or owners.
  • 5. No bypassing moderation: Avoid using tricks to bypass word filters or moderation systems.
  • 6. No self-promotion: Posting links to your own content is allowed in moderation but excessive self-promotion is prohibited.
  • 7. Follow the privacy policy: Respect users' privacy and do not share personal information.
  • 8. Follow all applicable laws: Do not use Zane.org to engage in illegal activities.
