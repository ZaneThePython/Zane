Community Rules

Please follow these guidelines to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience on Zane.org.

  • 1. Be respectful: No harassment, hate speech, or bullying.
  • 2. No spamming: Avoid excessive messages or advertisements.
  • 3. Keep it appropriate: No NSFW content or illegal activity.
  • 4. No impersonation: Do not pretend to be someone else.
  • 5. No bypassing moderation: Do not attempt to bypass word filters or security measures.
